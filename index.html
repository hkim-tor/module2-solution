Backyard birds

Northern cardinal

The northern cardinal is a North American bird in the genus Cardinalis; it is also known colloquially as the redbird or common cardinal. It can be found in southern Canada, through the eastern United States from Maine to Texas and south through Mexico, Belize and Guatemala.

Blue jay

The blue jay is a passerine bird in the family Corvidae, native to North America. It is resident through most of eastern and central United States, although western populations may be migratory.

American tree sparrow

The American tree sparrow, also known as the winter sparrow, is a medium-sized sparrow. It had been classified under the genus Spizella, but multilocus molecular evidence suggested placement in its own genus.
